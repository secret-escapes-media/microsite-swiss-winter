Luxury lounges

Zurich Lounge

Pure luxury awaits at the SWISS Zurich lounge at Zurich airport.

From two hotel rooms, conference room, mini suites, champagne bar and two restaurants, SWISS supplies everything you could possibly need. A wine humidor offers over 1000 bottles from all over the world, a perfect accompaniment to the à la carte selections on offer. The Senator Lounge has its own whisky bar with over 120 different tipples, and chefs are on call to cook in front of wherever you choose to rest. Just don’t forget about the flight!

Geneva Lounge

Feel pampered in the Geneva Lounge, for maximum comfort on your onward journey.

Kick back in the Geneva Lounge with personal service that always goes the extra mile. With the advantage of a short walk to your boarding gate, you’ll have plenty of time to enjoy a light meal before jetting off to your destination. If you’re on business, pop over to the E-Corner to catch up with your emails in peace. SWISS will happily cater to any needs you have.

Find out more

Ski Equipment Flies For Free

Don’t fork out extra when going on a ski-holiday; fly with SWISS who understand your needs. Ski equipment flies for free, making sure you have the best possible time in Switzerland’s famous resorts. As long as the equipment does not exceed your overall baggage allowance, there will be no extra cost incurred.
