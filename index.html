Summer In Switzerland

There isn’t a country in Europe that can get our wanderlust going like Switzerland does. We've brought together a Swiss A-team to show you why we love it so much. Why Switzerland?

Switzerland deserves more than a typical destination guide (the internet is already full of those). Secret Escapes want to give you a real under the skin feel for one of the most beautiful places in the world. We’re itching to show you just how spectacular it is in summer.

Secret Escapes and Swiss International Air Lines will also be bringing you exclusive offers on flights and hotels so there will be no excuse not to book your flight immediately.

With more than 170 weekly SWISS flights from £55 one-way*, it’s easy to make Switzerland your adventure playground this summer.
