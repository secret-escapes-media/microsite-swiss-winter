Wintery rural wonders set about the towering, snow capped peaks of the Alps signal arrival in Switzerland, a winter wonderland that at every turn is picture-postcard perfect.

From alpine tradition to modern urban flair, Switzerland seamlessly intertwines old and new, catering for a vast range of tastes. Snowy chalets and cosy mountain pubs, cable cars and cuckoo clocks – tropes all found on the mountainside, sat next to bustling cities and chic ski resorts packed with contemporary art, fine dining, boutique shopping and trendy rooftop bars. Switzerland is truly a place of variety, ready for any type of traveller. If your winter dream is sailing down mountainsides and winding down after in the hot tub, or wrapping up warm and exploring christmas markets and dipping into contemporary galleries, Switzerland accommodates.
